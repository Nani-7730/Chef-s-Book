Dumplings Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground chicken
  • 2 cups finely chopped napa cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped green onions
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon rice vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1 tablespoon grated fresh ginger
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 package dumpling wrappers
  • Water, for sealing dumplings
  • Vegetable oil, for frying

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the ground chicken, napa cabbage, green onions, soy sauce, rice vinegar, sesame oil, ginger, garlic, and black pepper. Mix well.
  2. To assemble the dumplings, place a wrapper on a clean surface and place a tablespoon of filling in the center. Dip your finger in water and moisten the edges of the wrapper. Fold the wrapper in half and seal the edges, pressing firmly to remove any air pockets. Repeat with remaining wrappers and filling.
  3. To cook the dumplings, heat a large skillet over medium heat. Add enough vegetable oil to coat the bottom of the skillet. When the oil is hot, add the dumplings and fry for 2-3 minutes until golden brown. Add 1/2 cup of water to the skillet, cover with a lid, and cook for 5-7 minutes until the dumplings are cooked through and the water has evaporated. Repeat with remaining dumplings, adding more oil and water as needed.
  4. Serve the dumplings hot with soy sauce or your favorite dipping sauce.
