Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, chilled and diced
  • 4 tablespoons ice water
  • 4 cups pitted fresh cherries
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tablespoon granulated sugar

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt. Cut in the butter until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. Stir in the ice water until the dough comes together. Shape into a disk, wrap in plastic wrap, and chill for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Roll out the pie crust and fit into a 9-inch pie dish.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the cherries, sugar, cornstarch, and almond extract. Mix well and pour into the pie crust. Dot with the melted butter.
  4. Cover the pie with the top crust, crimping the edges to seal. Cut several slits in the top to allow steam to escape. Sprinkle with sugar.
  5. Bake for 45 to 50 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the filling is bubbly.

Tips

  • Be sure to pit the cherries before using them in the pie.
  • For a more decorative top crust, use a lattice pattern or cut out shapes with a cookie cutter.
  • Serve with vanilla ice cream or whipped cream, if desired.
