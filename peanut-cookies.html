Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. In a large bowl, cream together butter, granulated sugar, brown sugar, and peanut butter until light and fluffy.
  3. Beat in egg and vanilla extract.
  4. In a separate bowl, whisk together flour, baking soda, and salt.
  5. Add the flour mixture to the peanut butter mixture and stir until well combined.
  6. Roll the dough into 1-inch balls and place them on a greased baking sheet.
  7. Use a fork to press down on the dough balls to flatten them slightly and create a criss-cross pattern on top.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes or until the cookies are golden brown.
  9. Remove from oven and let cool on the baking sheet for 5 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
