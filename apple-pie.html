Ingredients

  • 1 9-inch unbaked pie crust
  • 6 cups thinly sliced, peeled apples
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon milk
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Place sliced apples in a large bowl. In a small bowl, combine sugar, lemon juice, cinnamon, nutmeg, and salt. Sprinkle over apples and toss to coat. Pour apple mixture into pie crust and dot with butter.
  3. Top with second pie crust and seal and crimp the edges. Cut slits in the top of the pie to allow steam to escape. Brush top with milk and sprinkle with sugar.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly. Cool on a wire rack before serving.
