Spring-roll Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 package spring roll wrappers
  • 1/2 pound ground chicken
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1/2 cup chopped mushrooms
  • 1/4 cup chopped scallions
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix together the ground chicken, shredded carrots, shredded cabbage, chopped mushrooms, scallions, soy sauce, oyster sauce, cornstarch, vegetable oil, and black pepper.
  2. Place a spring roll wrapper on a flat surface and add 1-2 tablespoons of the chicken mixture in the center.
  3. Roll up the wrapper, tucking in the sides, and brush the edge with beaten egg to seal.
  4. Repeat with the remaining wrappers and chicken mixture.
  5. In a wok or deep-fryer, heat the oil to 375Â°F.
  6. Fry the spring rolls in batches for 2-3 minutes until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Drain the spring rolls on paper towels and serve hot with sweet chili sauce.
