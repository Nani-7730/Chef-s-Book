Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups granulated sugar
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 can (8 oz) crushed pineapple, drained
  • 1 cup chopped pecans

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. In a large bowl, beat eggs and sugar until well combined.
  4. Stir in oil, grated carrots, crushed pineapple, and chopped pecans.
  5. Add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and mix until just combined.
  6. Pour batter into a greased 9x13 inch baking pan.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  8. Let cool in the pan for 10 minutes before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.

Decoration

You can decorate the cake with your favorite frosting and toppings such as whipped cream, chocolate chips, or fresh carrots.
